# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer
by opening a private report through GitHub (a Security Advisory at
<https://github.com/mangetoncompost/mirage/security/advisories/new>, or a direct
message via the [@mangetoncompost](https://github.com/mangetoncompost) profile).
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response
appropriate to the circumstances. The maintainer is obligated to maintain
confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
